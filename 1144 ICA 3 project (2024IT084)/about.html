About Our Student Network

UniShelf was created to directly combat expensive retail markup pricing on mandatory course textbooks. We serve as a direct, peer-to-peer campus marketplace link.

Instead of storing old materials away in boxes where they lose relevance, list them on our community platform to make immediate pocket money.
